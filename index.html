About Me

I am a microbiologist from Colombia, graduated from the Universidad Industrial de Santander in Bucaramanga, Colombia (2022). Currently, I am pursuing a Master's degree in Microbiology with a focus on Virology (2023 - present).

My research experience includes participation in projects related to infectious diseases. During my MSc thesis, I worked on the molecular characterization of Respiratory Syncytial Virus and Rhinovirus, identifying their genotypes and clades through phylogenetic analysis. Additionally, I performed an in-depth molecular and phylogenetic analysis to infer the evolutionary history of these viruses, unveiling patterns of divergence, adaptation, and global and local dynamics.

My current research interests include virology, RNA viruses, respiratory viruses, and immune responses against viruses.
